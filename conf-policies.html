Conference Policies

A. NO-SHOW Policy

  • ICIIP-2025 implements a NO-SHOW policy. All accepted manuscripts must be presented at the conference by an author/co-author(s). All non-presented manuscripts will be considered under the “NO-SHOW” category. Once a manuscript is deemed no-show, it will not be sent to IEEE Xplore for further possible publication or other public access IEEE forums.
  • A non-author substitute presenter will be allowed only in the case of Indian participants under exceptional circumstances and must be pre-approved by the Conference Technical Program Chairs / General Chair(s). Requests for non-author substitute presenters must justify why none of the authors are able to attend and must certify that the substitute presenter (who needs to be identified by name and affiliation) will be able to satisfactorily present the work and respond to questions from ICIIP-2025 attendees. If the substitute presentation is not satisfactory in the assessment of the Session Chairs or Technical Program Chairs, the manuscript will be considered a no-show, irrespective of any prior pre-approval.
  • No-show manuscript authors wishing to appeal the non-presentation notification must appeal by email to the ICIIP-2025 Technical Program Chairs at iciip2025@juitsolan.in no later than 15 days before the start of ICIIP-2025, providing valid evidence to justify their absence.

Possible Evidence for Exceptions:

  • Medical documents
  • Death notices
  • Visa rejection letter

Special VISA Considerations:

  • Passport holders or residents of the following countries must complete their documentation in consultation with MHA and conference organizers:
    • Afghanistan, China, Iran, Iraq, Pakistan, Sudan
    • Foreigners of Pakistani origin
    • Stateless persons
  • In case of VISA refusal for accepted manuscripts from these regions, SKYPE presentation may be arranged.

B. Plagiarism Policy

  • ICIIP-2025 strictly implements a Plagiarism and Self-Plagiarism Policy. Plagiarism found at any stage of manuscript publication will be taken as a serious offense.

Plagiarism and Excessive Re-use of Author's Earlier Material:

  • Manuscripts must be original and must not be submitted simultaneously for consideration in another conference or journal. All submissions will be checked using iThenticate for plagiarism and self-plagiarism.

Similarity Index Explanation:

  • The similarity score represents the cumulative sum of similarity scores with all documents in the CrossCheck database.
  • Plagiarism in any form is unacceptable and will result in ethical and legal consequences. Corrective actions are defined under the IEEE PSPB Operations Manual (Section 8.2.4).
  • Authors are advised to keep their similarity score well below ≤20.

C. Withdrawal Policy

  • Manuscript withdrawal is possible at any time before acceptance.
  • Once a manuscript is accepted and an acceptance email has been sent, withdrawal is not permitted. The manuscript will be published in local conference proceedings.
  • Authors who wish to withdraw an accepted manuscript must discuss with the conference chair.

D. Change of Author(s) & Affiliation(s) Details

  • ICIIP-2025 strictly prohibits changing the sequence or details of authors after submission. The submitted author list in the review manuscript must match the final camera-ready version.
  • If any discrepancy or unauthorized change is noticed at any stage, the manuscript will be REJECTED and removed from the final proceedings.
