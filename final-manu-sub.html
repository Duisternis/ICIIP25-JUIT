Final Manuscript Submission

Instructions for the Authors of Accepted Papers

Authors are required to follow all the instructions listed under General and Required category for final submission of their manuscript to IEEE Xplore.

General Instructions:

  • Authors are required to remove header or footer (if there is any) in their Final Camera ready manuscript.
  • Do not put any page numbers in the Camera ready manuscript.
  • Remove the titles like (Prof., Dr., Mr., Ms., etc..) before the author name(s).
  • Remove hyperlinks (if there is any) in the final version of the manuscripts.
  • Format the paper using IEEE standard template.
  • In case you are using IEEE LaTeX template then use the following \documentclass code to change the Paper size from letter to A4:
\documentclass[a4paper, 10pt, conference]{IEEEtran}
