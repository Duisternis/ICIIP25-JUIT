IMAGE INFORMATION PROCESSING

(ICIIP-2025)

November 27-29, 2025

Manuscript Preparation

Authors are invited to electronically submit their paper using the specified IEEE format format written in English, of up to 6 pages (Full Paper) and 4 pages (Short Paper/ Poster). Full paper presenting the results of original research or innovative and short paper may have partially completed with theoretically established idea to practical applications relevant to the conference. All submissions are to be done electronically using Microsoft Conference Management Toolkit Submission link. The link for the same will be available soon. All accepted and presented papers will be sent to IEEE Xplore for their further possible publication and indexing with various databases like ACM Digital Library, DBLP, Compendex, ScienceDirect, Scopus, Google Scholar, INSPEC, JournalTOCs, CiteSeerX, Web of Science, Journal Citation Report(JCR), and ERIC etc.

Manuscript Submission

All manuscripts submitted to ICIIP 2025 will undergo a peer-review process, and the submitted manuscript will be reviewed by at least three reviewers.

Authors must ensure that their manuscript is in compliance with Manuscript Preparation Guidelines and can submit a MAXIMUM of 2 papers to ICIIP 2025 using the Microsoft Conference Management Toolkit Submission link:

https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/ICIIP2025

Authors can use MS Word A4 or LaTeX template for preparing their manuscript and check the PDF for compliance within EDAS conference system and for its further submission.

FORMAT GUIDELINES:

  • Paper size: The paper size for submission of manuscripts must be A4.
  • Paper format: Two-column format in MS Word or LaTeX style is mandatory.
  • Page limit: Manuscripts in PDF formats should not exceed 6 pages for full paper and 4 pages for short paper/poster.
  • PDF file: A manuscript file needs to be converted into PDF and must be compatible with Adobe Acrobat 5.x or higher version for further submission.
