Travel Information

Convenient airports:

  • Shimla (30 Km)
  • Chandigarh (100 Km)
  • IGI New Delhi (350 Km)
  • Amritsar (350 Km)

All buses from New Delhi (Kashmiri Gate ISBT), Chandigarh (ISBT, Sec 43), and other locations to Shimla drop and pick up passengers from Waknaghat (3 Km from Conference venue).

Direct trains from New Delhi, Delhi, and other locations are available up to:

  • Kalka (70 Km)
  • Chandigarh (100 Km)
  • Ambala (140 Km)

After that, taxis or buses are available to Waknaghat, Shimla. Alternatively, one can travel by Kalka-Shimla Heritage Toy Train up to Kandaghat (12 Km from the conference venue).
